You will need
- Heinz baked beans
- Butter
- Tomato kechup
- Cheddar cheese (grated)

Steps
- place beans in a pan
- heat until warm
- add a generous blob of butter
- squeeze in some kechup
- continue to heaat for 2 minutes

Serving suggestion
- toast some white toast loaf bread
- buttern generously
- spoon beans onto bread
- sprinkle cheese over beans

Enjoy :-)
